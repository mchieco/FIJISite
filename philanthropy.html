Philanthropy

The main philanthropy event of the year is the QTHON. Last year, we were the top fraternity in donations, excedding over $10,000. Because of this, we were able to fund and support a child. His name is Colin, and he has severe Autism. As a group, it was our honor to be able to make his day and his parents happy. For most of the brothers, it was a real eye opener, showing the power of helping a child in need. For this year, we are starting to fundraise already, looking to fund another child.

The FIJI philanthropy event for the fall semester was the Phi Gam Olympics. It went extremely well with $1,100 raised. Having other organizations compete for a good cause is what FIJI as a whole encourages. The winner of the event was ZBT, for fraternites, and Gamma Phi Beta, for serorities.
